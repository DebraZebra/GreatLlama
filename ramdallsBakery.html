Ramdall's Bakery

Rams are guaranteed

Ramdall has worked hard to hone his skills in baking. Everything from bread to pastry in countless bakeries under a million chefs. He dreams of having his very own bakery. After years of diligently saving Ramdall is about to get an amazing deal on a bakery -space that is already stocked with appliances. Ramdall could start baking on his first day! Ramdall dons his apron and heads to the bakery but what he sees when he arrives is a huge surprise- sentient tools, water overflowing from somewhere, loaves of bread waiting to go into the oven, knives on the floor, gigantic sticks of butter. Some things are gigantic, others are impossibly small. Unsure how to solve these bigger problems Ramdall begins with what he knows best- getting the bread in the oven. Navigate through a series of kitchens that get increasingly complex. Including angry oven mitts, cartwheeling whisks, butter boogie boards, refrigerators that fall from the sky and SO MUCH BREAD.
